Species Lists

Species Lists display the common and scientific (Latin) names of the birds found in the park.

Birds Found During Monitoring

This list displays all of the bird species found during the ongoing bird monitoring program. By default, the list includes all species from all points (sampling locations) in a park. If you want a list for specific points, delete "All Points" and select one or more points from the dropdown. The name of a point can be found by hovering over it on the 'Map' panel.

All Birds Known from the Park

This list displays all of the bird species in a park with an occurrence status of “present” or “probably present” based on existing evidence within NPSpecies, the master biodiversity database for the National Park Service. For more information, including species lists and occurrence status of other groups, visit the NPSpecies page.
