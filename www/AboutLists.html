Birds found during Monitoring

This list displays all of the bird species found during monitoring. By default, this is shown for all of the points in a park. If you want to see a list for only certain points, you can delete "All Points" from the "Points (optional)" control and choose one or more points. The name of a point can found by can be found by hovering over it on the 'Map' panel.

All Birds known from the Park

This is a list of all bird species believed to be present in the parks. This list is a subset of the data managed in NPSpecies, which is the master biodiversity database for the National Park Service. The species lists presented here have all gone through a certification process. This involves the evaluation and review of the species information by local experts who are well-versed in the biology, taxonomy, and nomenclature of species in the parks. For more information, including species lists of other groups, such as birds and mammals, visit the National Capital Region Network Species List page.
